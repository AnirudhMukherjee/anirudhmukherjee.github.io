Biography

Hey there! My name is Anirudh. I’m a recent graduate in Computer Engineering from the University of Mumbai.

My research interests lie in the fields of Computer Graphics and Artificial intelligence particularly encompassing the recent advancements in virtual environments, simulations, physical animations, realtime rendering and reinforcement learning. I also draw stuff every now and then, either through a physical medium or through code and doodle around on my guitar for hours at end.

I will soon begin my graduate studies in Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology in Fall, 2021.
